Welcome to BAMC 2027

The British Applied Mathematics Colloquium (BAMC) is the largest annual meeting of the applied mathematics community in the UK. The conference brings together researchers from across applied mathematics, industry, and related disciplines to share new ideas and developments.

BAMC 2027 will be hosted by the School of Computer Science and Mathematics at Keele University from 21–24 June 2027.

Key Dates

  • Mini-symposia proposals open: Autumn 2026
  • Early registration deadline: April 2027
  • Abstract submission deadline: May 2027
  • Conference dates: 21–24 June 2027

Conference Overview

The programme will include plenary lectures, mini-symposia, contributed talks, poster sessions, and opportunities for networking across the UK applied mathematics community.

Further information about speakers, programme, and registration will be announced soon.
